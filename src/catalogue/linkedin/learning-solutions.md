## Overview

LinkedIn Learning Solutions is a comprehensive enterprise learning management platform that combines LinkedIn's vast library of 25,000+ courses with advanced learning analytics and Microsoft 365 integration. Designed for large-scale organisational learning and development, it provides UK public sector organisations with the tools to build skilled, adaptable workforces.

### Content library

- **25,000+ courses** from industry-leading experts
- **700+ hours** of new content added monthly
- **Multi-language support** including English, Spanish, French, German
